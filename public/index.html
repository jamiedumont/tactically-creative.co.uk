Straight-talking digital design

Tactically Creative is how I help small businesses with their websites and apps. That "help" can range from occasional consultancy, all the way to creating a bespoke website from scratch.

I often help business' with their online strategy, and then build and manage their site as part of a "start-to-finish" service. However, if you'd prefer sit down over a coffee and thrash out your website's woes in an intense consultation, I'm game! Whatever you choose, you get access to all of my knowledge, skills and experience in a format that works for you.

Fundamentally, I want to give you the peace of mind to focus on your business; I'll take care of your site.

Who am I?

I'm Jamie Dumont, and I'm an odd hybrid of a designer, developer & a business owner (who can't stand the word "entrepreneur")... I not only build digital *stuff* for other people, but also myself and my own business.

I'm a great believer in what small, independent businesses can achieve, and the value that thoughtful digital design can offer them. I've decided to no longer work with any businesses that have taken any venture capital...
